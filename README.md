## 🔬 MS Thesis Research (NUST IGIS, 2013–2017)

> **"Influences of Land Use, Land Cover and Height of Sampler on Atmospheric Pollen and Spore Densities at NUST Campus, Islamabad"**

- Designed a **low-cost air sampler** for bio-aerosol collection across campus zones
- Investigated correlation between **LULC variables** (vegetation, built-up, soil) and pollen/spore densities
- Used **GIS spatial analysis** to model distribution patterns
- Collaborated with **Pakistan Meteorological Department** and NUST Environmental Sciences for species identification
- Tools: ArcGIS, ERDAS Imagine, ENVI, eCognition, HYSPLIT trajectory modelling
